# Publication Boundary for Marketplace Workflows

## 60-second summary

A practical human-in-the-loop rule: an assistant may prepare a public listing, organize images, fill fields, and check the preview, but the final publish action stays manual.

## Boundary

Allowed assistant support:

- draft title and description;
- organize photos;
- fill non-sensitive form fields;
- prepare pricing notes or comparison research;
- preview and check for mistakes.

Human-only final action:

- submit, publish, purchase, pay, or send a public marketplace listing.

## Why it matters

This is a concrete example of a broader AI operator rule: public-facing, payment-linked, or externally binding actions should remain behind a human approval gate even when most preparation can be assisted.

## Recommended wording

> Assistants can prepare marketplace workflows, but final publication remains a deliberate human action.
