# ADR 0001 — Single Orchestrator, Multiple Specialist Lanes

## Status
Accepted for the public case-study workflow.

## Context
AI-assisted work can involve multiple tools or model families. Without a single owner, scope, approval, and final decisions become fragmented.

## Decision
Use one orchestrator as the accountable front. Specialist lanes can plan, build, review, fact-check, or challenge, but they do not independently publish, deploy, or approve irreversible actions.

## Consequences
- Clear final ownership.
- Easier public communication.
- Lower risk of invisible automation.
- Requires explicit handoff packets and closeout summaries.
