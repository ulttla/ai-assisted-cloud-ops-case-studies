# ADR 0002 — Consensus Evidence Before Closeout

## Status
Accepted.

## Context
A summary of work is not the same as evidence that the work was reviewed. For infrastructure workflows, reviewers may disagree about risk, validation depth, or public-readiness.

## Decision
Important work should close with:

- lanes invoked
- what they agreed on
- what they disagreed on
- what was adopted and why
- validation performed
- remaining risk

## Consequences
- Better audit trail.
- Easier handoff after context resets.
- More honest portfolio claims.
- Slightly higher closeout overhead, justified for non-trivial work.
